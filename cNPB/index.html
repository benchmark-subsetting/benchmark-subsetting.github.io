NAS-C-OpenMP3.0

Most of the NPB 3.0 are written in Fortran. We propose an unofficial C version of the NPB 3.0 OpenMP that is built upon the NPB OMP 2.3 C version from the Omni Compiler Project . Informations on the original NAS Parallel Benchmarks are available at NASA NPB. The guideline for the translation from the 2.3 to the 3.0 was to match the parallel regions structure.
